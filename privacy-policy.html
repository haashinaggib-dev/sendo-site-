Privacy Policy for Sendo

Last updated: May 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Sendo (the "app") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the app on your iPhone or iPad. Sendo is built to be privacy-first: it does not run servers, does not synchronize your data to the cloud, and does not include analytics or advertising.

1. Information Sendo does NOT collect

  • Sendo does not have a server. No information about you, your messages, or your contacts is sent to the developer or any third party.
  • Sendo does not use analytics services (no Google Analytics, no Mixpanel, no Firebase, no Crashlytics).
  • Sendo does not display advertising and does not work with any advertising network.
  • Sendo does not collect device identifiers (IDFA, advertising ID) for tracking.
  • Sendo does not access your photos, location, calendar, health data, or any system data outside of the explicit permissions described below.

2. Permissions Sendo requests, and why

Microphone

Sendo asks for permission to use your iPhone's microphone only when you press and hold the record button to make a voice message. The recorded audio is stored temporarily on your device and is attached to a multimedia message (MMS) that is sent through Apple's standard message composer. Sendo does not upload your recordings to any server. You can revoke this permission at any time in iPhone Settings → Sendo → Microphone.

Contacts

When you tap "Pick from my contacts," Sendo opens Apple's native contact picker. You choose a single contact, and only that contact's name and phone number are returned to Sendo. Sendo never reads your full contact list and never uploads contact information anywhere. You can revoke this permission at any time in iPhone Settings → Sendo → Contacts.

3. Data Sendo stores on your device

Sendo stores the following data only on your local device:

  • Contacts you have added to Sendo (name and phone number).
  • A local copy of text messages you have sent from Sendo, so they appear in your chat history.
  • Metadata about voice notes you have recorded (duration, timestamp). The audio files themselves are temporary unless you re-record.
  • Your app preferences (e.g., notification settings, font size).

This data is stored in iOS local storage (LocalStorage) and is only accessible by Sendo on your device. If you uninstall the app, this data is deleted. If you reinstall, it does not return — Sendo has no backup or sync mechanism.

4. Messages sent through Sendo

When you send a message through Sendo, the app uses Apple's native MFMessageComposeViewController to prepare a standard SMS or MMS. You then tap "Send" in Apple's secure compose sheet, and your phone's carrier delivers the message in the normal way — exactly as if you had typed it in your Messages app. Sendo does not act as a middleman. Sendo cannot read replies that arrive on your phone, because Apple does not allow third-party apps to access incoming SMS.

5. Children

Sendo is not designed for or targeted at children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect any personal information from users of any age, as it does not collect personal information at all.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated when Sendo adds new features or when the app's behavior changes. Any update will be reflected on this page along with a new "Last updated" date. Continuing to use the app after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
