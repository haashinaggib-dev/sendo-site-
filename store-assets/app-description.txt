Sendo — The messenger without internet.

Reach the people who matter, anywhere, anytime — even where Wi-Fi and mobile data don't exist. Sendo brings a clean, modern chat experience to plain SMS and MMS, so your messages always get through.

Built for everyone with family or friends in remote places, low-coverage areas, or anywhere a cellular signal works but the internet does not.

— WHAT MAKES SENDO DIFFERENT —

NO INTERNET REQUIRED
Sendo doesn't use Wi-Fi or mobile data to send messages. Everything goes through the regular cellular SMS and MMS service your SIM card already supports. If you can make a phone call, you can send a Sendo message.

VOICE MESSAGES OVER SMS
Record and send real voice notes that arrive as MMS audio attachments on any phone — iPhone, Android, even basic feature phones. Your loved ones don't need WhatsApp, Signal, Telegram, or any app to receive your voice. They just open their normal messages.

BEAUTIFUL, FAMILIAR CHAT INTERFACE
A polished, WhatsApp-style chat experience for the SMS messages you already send every day. Bubbles, voice playback, contact info, delete options — everything you expect from a modern messenger, but powered by reliable SMS instead of fragile internet.

WORKS WITH ANY PHONE ON THE OTHER END
The person you message doesn't need Sendo. They don't need internet. They don't need anything special — just any phone with a SIM card. Your message arrives as a regular text or MMS in their default messages app.

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Sendo doesn't run servers. Your contacts and conversations stay on your device. No cloud sync, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising. Your messages travel through your carrier directly — the same way SMS has worked for thirty years.

— HOW IT WORKS —

1. Open Sendo and tap the green + to add a contact, either by picking from your phone's contact list or entering a name and number manually.

2. Tap the contact to open a chat. Type your message and tap send — Apple's secure compose sheet appears with your message already prepared. Tap send to deliver it through your carrier.

3. To record a voice note, press and hold the microphone, speak, and release. The audio is automatically packaged as an MMS attachment.

4. Replies arrive in your iPhone's regular Messages app — Sendo doesn't take over your inbox. You stay in control.
