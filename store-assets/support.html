Frequently asked questions

Does Sendo really work without internet?

Yes. Sendo sends messages through your phone's SMS and MMS service, which is part of your normal mobile plan. You do not need Wi-Fi, mobile data, or any internet connection. If your phone has a cellular signal, Sendo works.

Why does Sendo ask for "Cellular Data" to be turned on?

iPhones use the "Cellular Data" setting to enable MMS attachments (voice notes, photos). This is a setting Apple controls, not Sendo. Turning the setting on does not automatically consume data — it only allows MMS to travel through your carrier's MMS service, which is normally part of your SMS bundle.

Why do replies from my contacts appear in Messages instead of Sendo?

Apple does not allow third-party apps on iPhone to read incoming SMS. This is a security rule that applies to every messenger on the App Store, not just Sendo. Your replies arrive in iPhone's built-in Messages app, where you can read them. You can then return to Sendo to continue sending.

Does the person I'm messaging need Sendo too?

No. Sendo sends regular SMS and MMS, so the person on the other end can have any phone — iPhone, Android, or even a basic feature phone. They receive your message in their normal messaging app and do not need to install anything.

Are my messages and contacts safe?

Yes. Sendo does not run any servers. Your contacts and your sent messages are stored only on your iPhone, in local storage that only Sendo can access. We do not sync, back up, or share any of your data. We do not use analytics or advertising trackers. See our privacy policy for details.

How do I record a voice message?

Open a chat, then press and hold the microphone button on the right side of the input bar. While holding, you'll see a red recording indicator and a timer. Speak, then release to finish. Sendo will open Apple's compose sheet with the audio attached as an MMS — tap Send to deliver.

How do I delete a chat or contact?

On the home screen, press and hold a chat row for about half a second. A red "Delete chat" sheet will slide up. Tap Delete to remove that conversation and contact permanently from Sendo. Or open the chat → tap the contact at the top → tap "Delete conversation."

What does Sendo cost?

Sendo is a one-time purchase. There are no subscriptions, no monthly fees, and no advertisements. Once purchased, you can use Sendo on any iPhone signed into your Apple ID, forever.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple, not by the Sendo team. To request a refund, go to reportaproblem.apple.com within 14 days of purchase, find Sendo in your purchase history, and select "Request a refund."
